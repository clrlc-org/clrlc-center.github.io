About the Center

The Center for Low-Resource Languages & Cultures (CLRLC) is a research- and community-driven initiative dedicated to advancing linguistic research and language technologies for under-resourced languages and cultures worldwide.

We work at the intersection of linguistics, artificial intelligence, and community engagement, developing tools, datasets, and research frameworks that support linguistic diversity, cultural preservation, and equitable participation in the global AI ecosystem.

Our Mission

With over 7,000 languages spoken globally, many remain critically under-represented in digital technologies and AI systems. Our mission is to ensure that these languages and the cultures they embody are not excluded from technological progress.

CLRLC advances inclusive research, ethical data practices, and culturally grounded language technologies. Through international workshops, conference engagement, mentorship programmes, and collaborative projects, we foster global partnerships that strengthen capacity and visibility for low-resource languages.
