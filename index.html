About the Center

The Center for Low-Resource Languages & Cultures (CLRLC) is a global, community-driven initiative committed to advancing the representation of low-resource languages and cultures in artificial intelligence. Our mission is to improve collaboration among researchers, linguists, technologists, and other stakeholders to create innovative solutions that support and elevate underrepresented languages and cultural knowledge in AI. At CLRLC, we focus on language documentation, data curation, and applied research to ensure high-quality, accessible resources for AI development. CLRLC organises workshops, mentorship, and training programs, offering opportunities for knowledge exchange, skill development, and cross-disciplinary collaboration. Through connecting experts and nurturing talent worldwide, we aim to empower communities, drive research, and advance the inclusion of low-resource languages and cultures in the global AI landscape.

Our Mission

Our Mission Our mission is to advance the representation and inclusion of low-resource languages and cultures in artificial intelligence by fostering collaboration among researchers, linguists, technologists, and communities worldwide. We strive to create innovative solutions through language documentation, data curation, applied research, mentorship, and training programs. By connecting experts, nurturing talent, and promoting knowledge exchange, we aim to empower underrepresented communities and drive meaningful progress in AI for low-resource languages and cultures.

Research

CLRLC is committed to advancing research on low-resource languages and cultures. We aim to support studies in language documentation, data curation, and AI applications, and to share our findings through publications and collaborative projects as our work develops.
